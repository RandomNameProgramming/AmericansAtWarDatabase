The American Civil War

Information about various American groups during the American Civil War

Native Americans

Most Native Americans indirictly helped one side or the other. Those that were originally based in the SE were promised their land back.

African Americans

During the Civil War African Americans got many major wins. Apart from the Emancipation Proclomation, they were also allowed to openly join the army. This caused many to fight for the Union and for freedom.

Whites

Many families were split up and forced to fight their brothers. Many fighting for the Union disobeyed policies about not freeing slaves.
