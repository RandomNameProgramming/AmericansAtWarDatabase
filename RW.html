The Revolutionary War

Information about various American groups during the Revolutionary War

Neutrality

Many tribes, like the Oneida, declared Neutrality between both sides.

Escaped Slaves

During the Revolution many slaves would escape and run to the British to fight under the promise of freedom

Most of them ended up going back into slavery after the war.

Enslaved Patriots

During the Revolution there were also many enslaved blacks who fought for the US hoping to be free afterwards. Most of them wern't free either.

Free Blacks

There were also men who purchesed freedom and became warriors when the war started.

Men

Many men in New England formed the early Militias and took part in the groups that fought for Independance.

Those that were loyal to the British attempted to calm the patriots and frequently fled to British Terrortory once War broke out.

Women

Women that were patriotic frequently acted as nurses and tried to change a womens role in a potential new nation.
